Our Training Solutions

SETA and QCTO accreditation standards

At SpecCon, we provide high-quality training that equips individuals with the accredited qualifications needed to play a meaningful role in the South African skills development sector. Whether you want to become a facilitator, assessor, moderator, or advance into Skills Development Facilitator (SDF) expertise, our training helps you meet SETA and QCTO accreditation standards.

We offer training that aligns with SETA and QCTO accreditation standards, ensuring that you meet all compliance requirements in your chosen field. Our programs include:

Facilitator Training

Learn how to plan and deliver effective learning interventions in line with SETA requirements.

Assessor Training

Develop the ability to assess learners against unit standards, qualifications, and skills programs.

Moderator Training

Gain the skills to ensure assessments are fair, valid, and consistent across providers.

Specialist Skills Development Programs

Targeted training aligned with industry needs to strengthen professional growth.
