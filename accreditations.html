Accreditation Made Simple

Navigating the accreditation process with SETA and QCTO can be challenging, especially with the strict compliance requirements. At SpecCon, we make this process simpler by guiding facilitators, assessors, moderators, training providers, and Skills Development Facilitators (SDFs) through every step of accreditation and scope applications.

Whether you are entering the sector or expanding your professional scope, SDF ensures that you are fully compliant and industry-ready.
