About SpecCon Brokers Connect (SBC)

Empowering Skills Development Specialists & Connecting Opportunities SpecCon Brokers Connect (SBC) is a dedicated platform that bridges the gap between accredited facilitators, assessors, moderators, subject matter experts (SMEs), and business opportunities. We specialize in linking skilled professionals with training providers, corporate clients, and industry stakeholders, ensuring that expertise meets demand in South Africa’s evolving skills development landscape. Our Mission To create a seamless, efficient, and growth-driven ecosystem where accredited skills development specialists can thrive while supporting businesses in accessing top-tier talent for training and development needs.
