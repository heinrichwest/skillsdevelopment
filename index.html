    At SpecCon, we provide end-to-end Skills Development Facilitation (SDF) services that enable organizations to comply with legislative requirements, maximize grant funding, and build skilled, sustainable workforces.

    Our role as Skills Development Facilitators includes:

    • Conducting skills audits to identify training needs.
    • Preparing and submitting Workplace Skills Plans (WSP) and Annual Training Reports (ATR).
    • Liaising with SETA to ensure compliance and smooth submissions.
    • Advising on mandatory and discretionary grants to unlock training funding.
    • Aligning training with QCTO and SETA-accredited providers.

    Our mission is to create a seamless and compliant ecosystem where businesses can access SDF expertise, while professionals in training and development are empowered to deliver maximum impact.
